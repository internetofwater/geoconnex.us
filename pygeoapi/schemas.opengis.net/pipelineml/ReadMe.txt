OGC(r) PipelineML Conceptual and Encoding Model Standard (PipelineML)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

The OGC PipelineML Conceptual and Encoding Model Standard defines concepts
supporting the interoperable interchange of data pertaining to oil and gas
pipeline systems.

More information may be found at
 https://www.ogc.org/standards/pipelineml

The most current schema are available at http://schemas.opengis.net/
and all OGC schemas may be downloaded in a complete bundle from
http://schemas.opengis.net/SCHEMAS_OPENGIS_NET.zip

* Latest version is: http://schemas.opengis.net/pipelineml/1.0/
