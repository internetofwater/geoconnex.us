OGC(r) WCS 1.1 Processing Service Standard (WCPS)
-------------------------------------------------------------------

The OGC(r) Web Coverage Processing Service (WCPS) defines a protocol-independent
language for the extraction, processing, and analysis of multi-dimensional
coverages representing sensor, image, or statistics data.

More information may be found at
 http://www.ogc.org/standards/wcps
and
 http://www.ogc.org/standards/wcs
