OGC(r) GroundWaterML Standard
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

This OGC WaterML 2: Part 4 - GroundWaterML 2 (GWML2) standard describes a
conceptual and logical model for the exchange of groundwater data, as well as a
GML/XML encoding with examples.

More information may be found at
 http://www.ogc.org/standards/waterml

The most current schema are available at http://schemas.opengis.net/ .
